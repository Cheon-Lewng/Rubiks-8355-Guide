This page tells you what the big story words mean. You do not have to read this first. You can come here when you are curious.

---

## 🟨 The Sun

The Sun is the yellow center piece. It lives in the sky. The Sun likes to stay on top. The Sun helps the whole story make sense.

---

## 🤍 Petals

Petals are white edge friends. Petals like to sit near the Sun. Petals help us at the beginning. After the white cross is done, Petals rest.

---

## 🌼 The Daisy

A Daisy is made of Petals. Four Petals sit around the Sun. It looks like a flower. The Daisy is only for the beginning.

---

## 🏠 Home

Every friend has a home. A home is where the colors match the walls. When a friend is home, it feels calm. If a friend is not home yet, that is okay.

---

## 🕸️ The Net

A net is one layer of the cube. We lift the net up or bring it down. The net helps us catch friends safely. A net is never a tool. It is part of the cube.

---

## 🦋 Butterfly

A Butterfly is a friend in the sky. The sky is the top layer. Butterflies like to fly in and out. In early steps, we help Butterflies land. Later, Butterflies may still fly, and that is okay.

---

## 🚪 The Working Door

The Working Door is a special open place. Friends go in and out through the door. The door stays open on purpose. The door stays in the same place for the step you are in. Leaving the door open is a good thing.

---

## 🧱 The Heavy Bottom

The bottom of the cube can feel heavy. It feels like two layers stuck together. Sometimes we move the heavy bottom to help. This is gentle and safe.

---

## 🤫 The Quiet Friend

A Quiet Friend is one edge that stays still. Other edges move around it. The Quiet Friend helps us watch and learn. There is only one Quiet Friend at a time.

---

## 🐟 Fish

Fish are corner friends at the very end. Fish live in the water. The water is below the sky. We help Fish by doing a dance. Fish only swim in the last step.

---

## 💃 The Dance

The Dance is a friendly routine. It looks the same each time. The middle can look messy. The end is always safe. The Dance brings the cube back.

---

## ➕ A Plus

A plus looks like a cross. It has one friend in the middle. Four friends point out. You will make a white plus and a yellow plus.

---

## ✅ Done

Something is done when it looks calm. Colors match. Friends are home. If it does not look calm yet, it is not bad. It just means the story is still going.

---

## 🧠 Adult Notes (Optional)

- This page is a glossary, not an instruction set.
- Definitions reflect story meaning, not mechanical detail.
- Terms intentionally avoid cube notation or theory.
- “Heavy bottom” is framed as a sensory description, not a strategy.
- “Working Door” is relational and step-dependent by design.
- “One algorithm” is taught as one conceptual action (“catching”), with environment (sky vs water) determining motion.
- Contributors should not expand definitions into procedures.
