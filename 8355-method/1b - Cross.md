## STEP 1B – MAKE THE WHITE CROSS

### 🎯 Big Goal
Turn the daisy into a **white plus** on the bottom.

---

### 📖 The Story (no moves)
The Sun stays on top.

Each Petal has a color friend.
When they match, the Petal can go down.
A piece is **home** when its colors match the colors of the walls next to it.

The bottom feels heavy, like two layers stuck together.

---

### 👀 What to Look For
- A Petal near the Sun.
- The side color of the Petal.
- The center below it.
- When colors match, the piece is **home**.
- Home is where a piece belongs.

---

### 🧱 Rules for This Step
- Sun stays up.
- Match before moving.
- Petals end after this step.

---

### ✋ The Moves
1. Match colors.
2. Push the Petal down.
	1. Down always means moving the Petal away from the Sun.
3. Repeat for all four.

---

### ✅ Check When You’re Done
A white cross lives on the bottom.

---

### 🧠 Adult Notes
Bottom is treated as two layers together. No middle layer language.
