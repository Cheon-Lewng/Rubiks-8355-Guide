## STEP 1A – MAKE THE DAISY

### 🎯 Big Goal
Make a **flower** around the **Sun**.

Four white **Petals** will sit next to the Sun.

---

### 📖 The Story (no moves)
This is your first time meeting the cube.

Meet the **Sun**.<br>
The Sun is yellow.<br>
The Sun lives on top.<br>
The Sun does **not** move.

Now meet the **Petals**.<br>
Petals are white.<br>
Petals like the Sun.

Petals are special helpers.<br>
They help for a little while.<br>
Later, they will rest.

---

### 👀 What to Look For
- The **Sun** in the middle on top.
- **White Petals** on the top cube.
- Open spots next to the Sun.

You may see none for a while.<br>
That is okay.

---

### 🧱 Rules for This Step
- The **Sun always stays on top**.
- **White edges are called Petals**.
- We are not solving yet.
- Use **gentle moves**.
- Keep doing the **same choice** each time.

---

### ✋ The Moves (story‑based, physical actions)
1. Hold the cube with the **Sun on top**.
2. Put your right hand on the cube.
3. **Lift the side by your right hand**.
4. Lift it again.
5. Lift it one more time.

If a **Petal appears on top**:
- Gently turn the **top** until there is an empty spot next to the Sun.

If **no Petal appears**:
- Hold the cube firm.
- Turn the **heavy bottom** together.

Then start again.

Go slow.<br>
The cube is safe.

---

### 🔁 If Something Looks Weird
That’s okay.

If nothing changes:
- Keep going.
- Use the **same gentle lift**.

If a Petal leaves the top:
- That can happen.
- It can come back later.

Nothing is broken.

---

### ✅ Check When You’re Done
You are done when:
- The **Sun** is still on top.
- **Four white Petals** sit all around it.
- It looks like a **daisy**.

You made a flower.

---

### 🧠 Adult Notes (Optional)
- Step 1A introduces a **mechanical loop** through experience, not explanation.
- The repeated lift of the same side cycles edge orientations until a white edge faces up.
- When no petal appears after the lifts, moving the **bottom two layers together** advances the loop without analysis (treated as the “heavy bottom” in kid text).
- Direction does not matter as long as the mover is **consistent**.
- This step avoids case recognition entirely and builds confidence through visible progress.
- The daisy is **temporary**; it simplifies what comes next without requiring understanding here.

---

_The petals have done their job, and a new shape is waiting to grow._
