## STEP 1C – WHITE CORNERS (3 ONLY)

### 🎯 Big Goal
Help **three white corners** find their homes.

**Stop at three.**<br>
One white corner must stay out on purpose.

---

### 📖 The Story (no moves)
The **Sun** is still in the sky.<br>
It stays calm and still.

Now we meet something new.<br>
Say hello to the **Butterflies**.

Butterflies live in the **top layer**.<br>
That is the sky.<br>
They like to fly in and out.

Today, we help **three** Butterflies land safely.<br>
One Butterfly keeps flying.<br>
That open space is important.

---

### 👀 What to Look For
- A **white corner** in the **top layer**.
- This corner is now called a **Butterfly**.
- Look closely at the **white sticker**.

A Butterfly is ready **only when the white sticker faces you** above its **home**.
A Butterfly is **not ready** when the white sticker is **not facing you**.

That can look like two things:
- The white sticker is **looking at the Sun**.
- The Butterfly is **caught under the net**, not in the sky yet.

Both mean the same thing:<br>
This Butterfly is not ready to land.

---

### 🧱 Rules for This Step
- Solve **only three** white corners.
- **The fourth white corner stays unsolved**.
- Butterflies are **only in the top layer**.
- A Butterfly may be solved **only if white faces you**.
- Use **the same move every time**.
- Letting a Butterfly escape is **safe**.
- In this step, we use something called a **net**.
- A net is **one side of the cube** that helps us catch Butterflies.
- We choose a net **when we make the move**.
- Sometimes the net is on the right. Sometimes it is on the left.
- That’s okay.
- The net can change. The cube stays safe.

---

### ✋ The Moves (story‑based, physical actions)
1. Hold the cube so the **Sun is on top**.
2. Find a **Butterfly** with white.
3. Check the white sticker.
   - If it **faces you**, keep going.
4. **Catch the Butterfly**.
   - The Butterfly **moves out of the net’s path**.
   - Lift the **net** up.
   - The Butterfly flies into the net.
   - Bring the net back down.
1. One white corner is home.

If the Butterfly is **not ready**:
- We **let the Butterfly go**.
	- Lift the net up.
	- Gently turn the **top** so the Butterfly moves out of the net’s path.
	- Bring the net back down.

Now look again.

When the white sticker **faces you**,<br>
**catch the Butterfly** the normal way.

---

### 🔁 If Something Looks Weird
That’s okay.

If a Butterfly escapes:
- That is allowed.
- Butterflies like flying.
- Catch it again when it is ready.

If you feel stuck:
- Pause.
- Look again.
- Use the **same move**, calmly.

---

### ✅ Check When You’re Done
You are done when:
- **Three** white corners are solved.
- **One** white corner is still missing.
- The **Sun is still on top**.

**Stop here.**<br>
Leaving one spot open is a success.

---

### 🧠 Adult Notes (Optional)
- Step 1C introduces the **Butterfly** metaphor and the **single core move**.
- Only corners in the **top layer** are treated as Butterflies.
- Requiring the white sticker to face the cuber ensures consistent, safe insertion.
- The unsolved fourth corner **implicitly creates the Working Door** needed for later steps.
- Emphasize stopping after three as a **rule**, not a mistake.
- Escapes are expected; recovery is always the same move.

___

### 🧩 _“Well done, Butterfly Catcher! Three Butterflies are home, and one special space stays open for the journey ahead.”_
_Pausing here means you did this step exactly right._
